Commitment of Gargi Group to Foundry Industry !! Technical Symposium on ‘Fettling’ on 11th March’13

 

Name of Lecture : ‘Fettling’
Main Speaker : Mr. Andrew Sparrow, UK
Venue : Auditorium of Rajkot Engg. Association
Attendance : 50 delegates

 

IIF Rajkot Chapter organized a Technical Symposium in association with Gargi Engineering Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. on ‘Fettling’ at Rajkot Engg. Association Hall on 11th March 2013. The main speaker was Mr. Andrew Sparrow, Sales Director, Clansman Dynamics, UK.
 

Mr. Andrew Sparrow gave technical presentation on Fettling in foundry development. He emphasized on the automation of the cleaning activity of the different sizes of the castings. He also showed how the mechanization can give uniform efficiency from start to end of the shift. More than 50 members had participated in the program.
