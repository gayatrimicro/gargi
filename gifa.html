GIFA 2007-The Foundry Family  
   

GIFA 2007 is over – and it really was an exciting and interesting time. This year saw more than 77,000 trade visitors from all parts of the world, 8% more than in 2003. The proportion of foreign visitors rose to 50 percent and over 1,700 companies, associations and research institutes presented the latest innovations in the areas of foundry technology, castings, metallurgy and thermal process engineering. The HA-Group stand in hall 3 always seemed to be the busiest place in the whole exhibition and we received many compliments on our performance.

 
The individual design and its consequent implementation, the “foundry family” theme and the reduced exhibits were praised by many visitors and led to numerous interesting technical dialogues, future-oriented projects and plenty of new contacts and friendships.
 

It was a wonderful mixture of languages and nationalities and every guest found a capable contact.The exhibition was a big success for the international HA family, based on the competence, teamwork and friendliness of the HA-Group members. We want to say Thank you ! and look forward to 2011!
