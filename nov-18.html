301+ Omega Installations in India

To celebrate on occasion of 301+ Omega Installations in India, Gargi Engineering Enterprises, Mumbai, hosted a dinner at Hotel The Residency, Coimbatore on Thursday, 15th Nov.'18. The dinner was attended by more than 175 foundrymen from leading foundries in Coimbatore and surrounding area.

Mr. Vinod Kapur, Executive Chairman of Gargi Group welcomed the guests and shared with them about Gargi's association with M/s. Omega Sinto Foundry Machinery and highlighted the following :

  • The agreement with Omega signed on 23rd Feb. 1994 when Late Mr. Arthur Ferns was the Managing Director and later on it was bought over by Mr. Mark Fenyes.


  • When we started selling Mixers, we realized that we should have supply of Resins and we signed collaboration with M/s. Huttenes Albertus Chemische Werke, Germany and started production during the year 1989. That was the beginning of introduction of Chemically Bonded Sand System in India.


  • Mr. Mark Fenyes has played a very important role in educating the Indian Foundrymen on Chemically Bonded Sand System and low cost mechanization, etc.


  • It was not only selling the equipment, but we at Gargi have sold the system.


Mr. S.K. Ahuja, Managing Director of M/s. Quality Castings, Fujairah briefed about his association with Omega since the year 1987 and the major reason of the technology with Reclamation, which was first installed by him at Nitin Castings. He mentioned about the advantages of being low cost investment, installation time, building cost, lower cost than conventional co2 system.

Mr. Ahuja along with Late Mr. Arthur Ferns and later on with Mr. Mark Fenyes initially worked very hard for the installation and giving practical knowledge to the foundrymen. With the new technology of equipment, Omega's equipment has become most popular with the foundrymen.

He also emphasized that Gargi HA has developed resin suitable for all product mix and are pioneer to provide not only resin but technology backed by expert service team with the expertise of Mr. Biswajit Ghosh, Technical Director, Gargi Engg., who has been involved in by 1st Omega Installation in India.

Mr. C.R. Swaminathan, Past President-IIF was 'Guest of Honour' at the event. He thanked Gargi HA for their regular support to the foundrymen in Coimbatore and organizing Technical Seminars and Programs.

Mr. Andy Pickering, Area Sales Manager, Omega Sinto spoke about today's Omega in India. He said after 30 years operation in India, today Omega Sinto has entire range of solution for No Bake foundries, whether it is small or large. Proven new technology use for Green Sand Reclamation of Cold Box use is added attraction to all. India is now more conscious about the environment.

During the event, foundrymen of Coimbatore have celebrated Mr. Vinod Kapur's 75th birthday, which was held recently.

Mrs. Sudha Kurupp, Executive Director of Gargi Engg. had thanked all our friends and well wishers for their presence and making the event a grand success.
