M/s. Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd., Mumbai was honoured Special Award under the Category : Medium-Chemicals Sector for the overall productivity performance. This award is set up by National Productivity Council, Mumbai (under Dept. of Industrial Policy & Promotion, Govt. of India) in co-operation with Dept. of Industries, Govt. of Maharashtra.

 

The award was presented to Mr. Vicky Kapur, Jt. Mg. Director & Mr. Gaurav Kapur, Director of Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd. by Mr. A.M. Khan, Principal Secretary for Industries, Govt. of Maharashtra . This function was held at Sahyadri Guest House , Mumbai on Thursday, the 20th August’09.

Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd., has won "Second Award" under 'Basic Inorganic & Organic Chemicals incl. Agro Chemical Panel' - SSM for the outstanding Export Performance for the year 2005-06. This award was set up by CHEMEXCIL (Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Govt. of India).

 

The award was presented to Mr. Vinod Kapur, Chairman & Mg. Director of Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai by Hon'ble Minister of State for Commerce & Industry, Govt. of India, Shri Jyotiraditya Scindia on occasion of presentation of 35th & 36th Export Awards. This function was held at Hotel ITC Grand Maratha, Sahar, Mumbai on Thursday, the 23rd July’09.
