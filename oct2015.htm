HAPPY MOMENT PROGRAMME NEWS IN NEWS PAPER      
       

At Gargi HA, Social Responsibility is a Core Thought, not an after thought.

      

 

With the help of Grampanchayat, Atkergaon, the team of Gargi HA (team consist of Mr. Vicky Kapur, Mr. Narendra Gavade, Ms. Rano Kapur & Ms. Poonam Thapar) visited the ‘Raigad District Municipality School’ on 22nd July’15 and discussed with Teachers and identified the students’ requirements. Based on it, we decided to give initially the following types of objects for the school children.

1. Uniform to Nursery
2. Shoes to all students
3. Educational CDs to all class
4. Safety Cover on Well
5. Tree Plantation.

 

On 27th July’15, we have distributed Educational CDs, Shoes and Colours. We also arranged Safety Cover on the well.

 

On 07th Oct 15, Gargi HA arranged Drawing & Writing Competitions for the students of ‘Raigad District Municipality School’, Atkargaon. During the competition, we provided all the required materials for Drawing, Writing and Sapling. After the competitions, prizes were distributed to the winners.

 

During the program we had also distributed Uniform to the students. Also we did tree plantation around the school premises.

 

Overall the program was very successful and there have been excellent response from students, Teachers and the villagers. This could have been possible due to the support we received from the Village Sarpanch, Mr. Sandeep Patil.

 

The event was published in the local newspaper. We look forward to take more projects in Environmental and Health Care with Social, Cultural and Educational program.
