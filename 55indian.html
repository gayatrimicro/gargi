55th Indian Foundry Congress, Agra - India
     

Gargi HA attended the 55 th Indian Foundry Congress and IFEX 2007 (largest Indian exhibition on foundry technology) which were both held at Hotel Jaypee Palace in Agra, India from February 2 - 4, 2007 and was organized by the Northern region of “The Institute of Indian Foundry men (IIF)”.
 

The Indian Foundry Industry is taking giant strides to meet global challenges

 
and the show was indeed a gateway to global markets.
 
The Indian Foundry Industry is taking giant strides to meet global challenges and the show was indeed a gateway to global markets. More than 1,000 delegates attended the Congress and more than 4,000 visitors attended the exhibition with 144 exhibitors, including International exhibitors from 9 different countries.
