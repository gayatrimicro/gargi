Commitment of Gargi HA to Foundry Industry !!
Gargi HA Binder Technology Series at Vadodara, Indore
By Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd., Mumbai.

 

IIF-WR alongwith Vadodara and Indore Chapters conducted IIF-WR Gargi-HA Binder Technology Series – Cast With Knowledge on 8th and 29th June, 2013 at Hotel Tansha Residency and Hotel Amarvilas respectively.

Through out the year, IIF-WR conducted the workshops on Binder Technology at various chapters to bring awareness about latest binder technologies, to introduce correct usage practices and reduction of rejection by correct identification of casting defects.

 

Vadodara:

 

Mr. S.I.D. Zion (Gen. Manager-Technical of Gargi Huttenus Albertus Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai) and Mr. N. Ramamurthi (Sr. Foundry Expert, Ahmedabad) conducted the workshop. Mr. Sanjay Ambast (Zonal Manager, M/s. Gargi-HA) assisted in replying the various queries by the participants and explaining the subject in local context.

Various topics covered were: Classification of Binders, Types of Silica Sand and its effects, Details about Alphaset Binder Technology, Applications, Process Control, How to get optimum performance, Handling of Alphaset Binders, Trouble Shooting, Identification and remedies of casting defects attributed to sand for cast irons.

25 enthusiastic foundrymen participated in the workshop and took advantage of the vast knowledge of both the faculties.

 

Indore:

 

IIF-WR Gargi-HA Binder Technology Series workshop at Indore was organised before the Annual General Meeting of IIF Western Region making a closure of the current team’s tenure with another useful programme.

Mr. Amish Panchal, Chairman-WR inaugurated the workshop, giving information about the various workshops under this series organised through out the year. He thanked M/s. Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt. Ltd. for supporting the activities of WR.

At Indore considering the request of the chapter, workshop covered on Cold Box Binder Technology.

Workshop was conducted by Mr. C. Shashidhara (Dy. Gen. Manager-Tech., M/s. Gargi Huttenus Albertus Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai) and Mr. N. Ramamurthi (Sr. Foundry Expert, Ahmedabad). Mr. Sanjay Ambast (Zonal Manager, M/s. Gargi-HA) assisted in replying the various queries by the participants and explaining the subject in local context.

Topics covered were : Classification of Binders, Types of Silica Sand and its effects, Details about ColdBox Binder Technology, Applications, Process Control, How to get optimum performance, Handling of ColdBox Binders, Trouble Shooting, Identification and remedies of casting defects attributed to sand for Cast Irons.

The workshop was attended by 65 foundrymen who took active interest and interacted for about 60 minutes with both the speakers to solve their problems and to gather more information at the end of the presentations.

IIF-WR Gargi-HA Binder Technology Series is supported by M/s. Gargi Huttenus Alburtus Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai.

With Workshop at Indore, Chairman Mr. Amish Panchal declared the IIF-WR Gargi HA Binder Technology Series completed.
