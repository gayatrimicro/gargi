Metal China 2015: Technology Innovation for Sustainable Development      

On March 29-30, the 7th CEO Forum on China Foundry Industry themed with "technology innovation and sustainable development" was held in Shanghai Everbright Hotel. There were more than 600 representatives including CFA members and other enterprises, especially the President of World Foundry Organization (WFO) Mr. Vinod Kapur, as well as the President of Japan Foundry Society Mr. H Kimura, attended the forum to discuss the foundry industry development in the New Normal situation.

The President of WFO Mr. Vinod Kapur made an analysis on the World Foundry Industry's situation with abundant and accurate data of 30 member countries. He also put forward a lot of relevant questions to promote the sustainable development of World Foundry Industry.

From March 31 to April 3, "Metal China 2015" and the "11th China International Die Casting Industry Exhibition", sponsored by China Foundry Association, were concurrently held in Shanghai. Mr. Vinod Kapur, President-WFO inaugurated the exhibition. There were 1,026 exhibitors from China mainland and Taiwan, as well as more than 20 countries including Germany, the United States, Japan, Italy, Turkey, etc. covering the nearly 70,000 square meters exhibition area. The exhibition received 61,349 person-time visits in four days.

There were more than 230 exhibitors of die castings manufacturers, die casting equipment and die suppliers, as well as relevant auxiliary products manufacturers.
