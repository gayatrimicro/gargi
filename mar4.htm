Safety Week Report
43rd National Safety Day / Week

 

The 43rd National Safety day / week was celebrated on 4th March 2014 at both GHA Nerul and Khopoli. The day saw a culmination of a week full of activities such as hazard identification, fire fighting and mock drills, rapid-fire quiz etc. Employees and workers from all departments in both Gargi HA units actively participated in the proceedings. Prizes for “safest plant”, “safest worker” and appreciation certificates were distributed.This year the participant numbers from both plants saw an increase of more than two fold from the previous year thus highlighting the commitment of the management and the support it has rendered towards EH&S. The Safety department also launched a scheme to increase worker and employee awareness and involvement by introducing toolbox talks, unsafe act identification, housekeeping as part of a points based system. On the whole the 43rdSafety day / week was very well appreciated by all involved. .
