Innoculants for High Duty Grey Iron & S.G. Iron
 
Well selected inoculant will refine grains; will provide more nucleating sites promoting more Graphite and not Iron Carbide precipitation and this in turn will improve Mechanical and Metallurgical Properties and Machinability of castings.

 

Today a very wide range of Inoculants are available using Ferrosilicon as a base in which varying degrees of elements which promote nucleation are added - either by mechanical blending or by casting as Single alloy.

 

Gargi has following range of Inoculants

A. Blended Inoculants: Garinoculant 'S', 'SB'; '10M' :Typically CaSi and /or Synthetic Graphite is Mechanically blended with foundry Grade FeSi ; these are low cost inoculants for low Pouring Temp. application and are effective in a limited way as the performance may not be consistent.

 

B. Single Alloy or Cast Inoculants: Ca,Al,Ba,Zr and Sr are most commonly used elements which are 'cast' into FeSi to create a family of very consistently effective Inoculants.

Gar Supanoc: Single alloy inoculant with guaranteed Ca and Al%; this is far more consistent than Blended Inoculants.

Garinoculant SBC: FeSi doped with varying amounts Ba and Manganese;very effective for all general purpose application; provides longest fading time;specially useful in pouring very large castings;moderate price and hence very popular.

Garinoculant ZR: FeSi doped with Zr as primary element and for special applications Ba and Mn are also added; very effective even with low bath sulphur; good for grain refinement.

Garinoculant SR: FeSi doped with Sr; most potent inoculant;provides excellent chill reduction in thin sections; lowest dross formation amongst all inoculants; widely used in critical Grey Iron auto components.

Metal Filtration
 
These are cellular ceramic filters that offer a simple effective method of removing slag, dross a non-metallic inclusions and promoting smooth metal flow. They are easy to use in existing casting systems by being inserted into the pouring or gating systems with minimum mould modification.
 
 
 
De-gassers, De- Oxidisers & Grain Refiners for Non-Ferrous
 
Aluminium Alloys

 

Garkover
A range of covering fluxes for all Aluminum & Zinc alloys including sodium free varieties. Suitable for all types of melting practices. They refine the metal, reduce Oxidation losses, minimize metal inclusion in drosses, modify hypoeutectic Aluminium silicon alloys and recover metal from drosses and scrap.

Gardegassers
A variety of tabletted degassing agents to eliminate porosity due to gases, promote modification and some grain refinement. Suitable for virgin Aluminium and its alloys.

 

Garnucleant
Tableted nucleating agents for different A1. Si alloys upto hypereutectic qualities to include grain refinement in the microstructure for superior physical properties.

 

Garcastal
Powder additive to eliminate shrinkage defects in Aluminium gravity diecastings.
