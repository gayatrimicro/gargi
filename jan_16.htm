Gargi HA Stall - A meeting Point for Indian & Overseas Foundrymen      

From 29-31 January 2016, GARGI HA Stall (Stall No. B-10, Hall B) was the Meeting Point for Foundrymen from all parts of India and Abroad @ IFEX-2016. Over 7000 visitors @ IFEX’16 and more than 1700 registered delegates of 64 Indian Foundry Congress emerged as the largest and most attended Foundry Industry Exhibition in India.


Members of the Kapur Family and other team members of Gargi HA were busy welcoming the delegates and discussing about their Products / Technologies and exchanging thoughts and information. The exhibit stand showcased various Cores & Castings from several key customers. Mr. Vinod Kapur, Mr. Vicky Kapur & Dr. Carsten Kuhlgatz were making special efforts to welcome Foundrymen from neighboring countries to develop deep understanding and strong business relations among the Foundrymen of this region.

 

GARGI is rapidly becoming a complete solution provider to the Foundry Industry.
