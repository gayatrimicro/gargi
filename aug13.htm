Ahmedabad:
   
   
 
   

WR Chairman, Mr. Anant Bam informed members about IIF-WR’s various projects, programme & targets.

Mr. Nishikant Ejrekar, Gen.Manager-Tech. Gargi HA covered practical tips on Coating Technology, Dos & Don’ts.

Mr. S. Subramaniam, Foundry Consultant, Pune covered Compatibility of Sand Systems with coatings, Defects due to sand system and Coatings, Corrective Actions related to coatings.

Programme was attended by 55 foundrymen at Ahmedabad.
 

 

Rajkot:

 

IIF-WR Gargi-HA Coating Technology Series programme at Rajkot Chapter was successful with 35 participants taking the benefit of the knowledge and experience shared by faculties Mr. Nishikant Ejrekar, Gen.Manager-Tech., Gargi HA Pvt. Ltd and Mr. S. Subramaniam, Foundry Consultant, Pune.

WR Chairman Mr. Anant Bam informed the Vadodara Chapter members about the activity plans and different projects of WR.
