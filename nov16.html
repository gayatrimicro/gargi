Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry !!
Gargi HA Binders & Coatings Technology Series at Kolhapur

Commitment to the Foundry Industry !! To continue having dissemination of technical knowledge by conducting seminars at various Foundry Clusters, Gargi HA had organized Technical Seminar on 9th November 2016 at Hotel Vrishali Executive, Kolhapur.

Purpose of this meeting was to understand customer specific requirement and to develop suitable Cold Box System. This is in line with response to the global requirement to reduce pollutant emissions from motor vehicles and the overall trend towards manufacture of smaller yet more powerful combustion engines.

Mr. Peter Groening, Product Manager-Cold Box Process, Huttenes Albertus, Germany was the key speaker on the new development of Cold Box System internationally. He was ably assisted by Mr. Vinayak Ghotge, Gen. Manager-Technical, Gargi HA on the new development of specially designed Refractory Coating.

At the event, Mr. Gaurav Kapur, Executive Director & Mr. Raju Somani, Vice President-WR, Gargi HA welcomed all participants and guests.

Mr. Sanjay Saraf, Associate Vice President (Operations), Menon & Menon Ltd. was the Chief Guest for the event. In his brief address, he shared several important points and useful information with the participants.

The seminar was conducted by :
  1. Mr. Peter Groening made a technical presentation on Innovative Cold Box Binder Solutions for Highly Complex Castings. He narrated about the developments done by HA in Cold Box Binder System to meet the increasing demands of new generation Core Shooters, which will meet foundrymenâ€™s stringent and specific requirements.

    He also recited on the use of special sands, engineered sand additives to be used with Silica Sands for making critical Cold Box Cores like port cores, water jacket cores of block and heads, etc.

  2. Mr. Vinayak Ghotge presented the latest development done at Huttenes Albertus on specially designed Refractory Coatings having unique rheological properties, good refractoriness/insulation, etc.., which will enable foundrymen to get clean internal/critical passages and veining free castings.

The event was attended by more than 90 foundrymen from Kolhapur and surrounding areas.
