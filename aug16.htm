Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry !!
Gargi HA Binders & Coatings Technology Series at Rajkot

Commitment to the Foundry Industry !! To continue having dissemination of technical knowledge by conducting seminars at various Foundry Clusters, Gargi HA in join hands with M/s. Western Minerals (Dealer for Rajkot area for Gargi's products) had organized Technical Seminar on 6th August'16 at Hotel Imperial Palace, Rajkot

Purpose of this seminar is to bring awareness about latest Binder technologies, to introduce correct usage practices and reduction of rejection by correct identification of casting defects.

At the event, Mr. Gaurav Kapur, Executive Director, Gargi HA and Mr. Jitendra Doshi, Founder, Western Minerals welcomed all participants and guests.

Mr. Amish Panchal, Vice President-IIF was Chief Guest for the event. In his brief address, he shared several important points and useful information with the participants.

The seminar was conducted by :
  1. Mr. S.I.D. Zion, Sr. General Manager-Technical, Gargi HA made a technical presentation on Resin Binders Technology, their characteristics, applications and care to be taken for their successful use.
  2. Mr. Vinayak Ghotge, Gen. Manager-Technical, Gargi HA shared technical information on various Refractory Coatings, their main compositions, methods of application, etc.

Mr. M.R. Shah was Moderator and Guest of Honour during the event. The event was attended by more than 250 foundrymen who took active interest and interacted with both the speakers to solve their problems and to gather more information at the end of the presentations.

Program was ended with vote of thanks by Mr. Ashit Doshi.
