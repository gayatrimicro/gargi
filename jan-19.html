Gargi HA Stall - A meeting Point for Indian & Overseas Foundrymen

From 18-20 January'19, GARGI HA Stand (Stand No. C-02, Hall 14 @ IFEX-2019) was Meeting Point for Foundrymen from all parts of India and Abroad. Kapur Family, HA team from Germany and Gargi HA team were making special efforts to welcome Foundrymen worldwide to develop deep understanding and strong business relations among the Foundrymen.

Mr. Mark Fenyes-Chairman and Mr. Andy Pickering, Area Sales Manager of Omega Sinto Foundry Machinery Ltd., U.K. along with Mr. Nitin Sane, Managing Director of Omega Sane Foundry Machinery Ltd., Pune welcomed foundrymen and explained them about the use of various types of Continuous Mixers, Sand Reclamation and Green sand. They explained in detail about the advantages/ benefits of the system

Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd. - A leading manufacturers of Foundry Resins & Coatings - is a joint venture company with M/s. Huttenes-Albertus Chemische Werke GmbH, Germany, who are among the world leaders in technology for Foundry Resins and Coatings, having their plants in 26 countries around the world.

Gargi Engineering Enterprises Pvt.Ltd. is in operation for more than 38 years. It represents many leading manufacturers of Foundry Plant, Machinery and Materials from India and abroad. Some of these companies are :

a. Omega Sinto Foundry Machinery Ltd., U.K.
b. Omega Sane Foundry Machinery Ltd., Pune
c. Kirloskar Pneumatic Co. Ltd., Pune

Display : Exhibited 10 Ton / hour Continuous Sand Mixer along with digital display and PLC Controlled Control Panel.

With the above profile, GARGI is rapidly becoming a complete solution provider to the foundry industry.
