Company Profile
At the beginning of the 1980s, an entire industry exploited the simple realization that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

 

The birth of Gargi Industries in 1960 not only marked the creation of India's largest centre for foundry auxiliaries; it was moreover the birth of the idea at the very heart of today's Gargi Group. Gargi Industries was born to form the foundations of a new industry philosophy, which was to change from supplier to active, solutions-oriented partner. This development was not only welcomed, it was put to the test, too. It shaped us into a corporate group which researches, improves, checks, renews, corrects and even revolutionizes in line with environmentally friendly foundry chemistry and metallurgy, whilst always bearing market demands and environmental issues in mind.

 

Over the past 5 decades the group has grown worldwide to maintain market leader in the foundry binder and auxiliary products market.

 

Gargi has many modern production plants in India and UAE. The company employs 700 people in India.

 

Amongst our nationwide customer base we count foundries of all major automotive companies, pump and machinery manufacturers and other industrial casting producers.
