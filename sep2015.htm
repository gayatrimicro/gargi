Casting Technology Workshop India : 2015

Organized by Continental Automotive Components India Pvt. Ltd

      

 

Gargi HA have been conducting workshops on Binder and Coating technologies at various foundry centers’ in our country to introduce correct usage practices and reduction of rejection by correct identification of Castings defects.

 

On Wednesday, the 2nd September, 2015 M/s. Continental Automotive Components India Pvt. Limited, organized a one day workshop titled “CASTING TECHNOLOGY WORKSHOP INDIA : 2015”, at Hotel Clarens, Gurgaon, New Delhi.

 

The workshop was organized specially for the Casting Vendors and Casting development team of Continental. 35 delegates participated in the workshop. The speakers were from eminent organizations of foundry equipments, foundry consumables and foundry software fields.

 

In foundry consumables, M/S. Gargi Huttenes Albertus Private Limited, Mumbai, conducted the workshop. Mr. Viney Luthra, GM (North) narrated Gargi HA group profile. Mr. C. Shashidhara, Gen. Manager & Technical Product Head-Cold Box Resin , presented on latest trends in Cold Box Binder System do’s and don’ts. Mr. Vinayak Ghotge, Gen. Manager & Technical Product Head-Coatings presented on latest offerings in Refractory Coatings, Casting Defects and its remedies through Refractory Coatings.

 

From Continental Mr. Joachim Kahn, Global Head-Casting Quality Management and Mr. Mustafa Ata, Senior Technical Expert Castings were present at the workshop.

 

The workshop was well received from all the participants.
