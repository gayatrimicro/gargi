Commitment of Gargi Group to Foundry Industry !! Technical Symposium on ‘Fettling’ on 13th March’13

 

Name of Lecture : ‘Fettling’
Main Speaker : Mr. Andrew Sparrow, UK
Venue : Residency Club, Kolhapur
Attendance : 125 delegates from Kolhapur Chapter

 

IIF Kolhapur Chapter organized a Technical Symposium in association with Gargi Engineering Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. on ‘Fettling’ at Residency Club, Kolhapur on 13th March 2013. The main speaker to the event was Mr. Andrew Sparrow, Sales Director, Clansman Dynamics, UK.
 

The program started with the welcome speech by Mr.Sanjay Karkhanis, Chairman IIF Kolhapur Chapter. V. Chairman Mr.Surjitsing Pawar gave introduction of speaker to all delegates. The program was well attended by large number of delegates in and surrounding areas from Kolhapur, Sangli, Ashta & Ichalkaranji.
 

Mr. Andrew Sparrow gave a good presentation on above subject. First he gave introduction of his company M/s. Clansman Dynamics, a world leading manufacturer and supplier of Foundry Manipulators & Robots. It produces manipulation with capacity upto 11000 Kg both static & Rail bound. Clansman Dynamics also produces manual grinding manipulators for fettling casting from few Kilos to over 50000kg and also produces the world’s largest range of air operated riser removal hammers.


The speaker focused on various product ranges like Manipulators, Canons, Powerbreakers, Grinders & Cutters etc. He also said that Clansman has a wide range of specifically designed and robust foundry solutions. He added that the design philosophy is simple… robust and reliable! This is achieved through close attention to the reduction and where possible the elimination of fatigue and stress failures. He stated that superior reliability and maximum simplicity means more uptime, lower maintenance costs, and smoother production. He also showed some live videos of foundry robots.
 

In an interactive session the speaker clarified all the queries raised by the participants on the subject.
 

The program concluded with vote of thanks by Mr.Samir Parikh, Hon. Treasurer of IIF Kolhapur Chapter. The program was extremely successful and appreciated by all the participants.
