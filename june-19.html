Press Release - GIFA 2019
HA Group Stand (Hall No. 12 Stand C 50 @ GIFA-2019) under the motto
“Foundry Inside ” was the Meeting Point for worldwide foundrymen to discuss with each other the challenges and opportunities which diverse worlds of foundrymen.
Huttenes Albertus (HA) presented a holistic concept for partnerships and co-operation with its partners at the stand. More than 30 of the HA Group’s subsidiaries and trading partners were present. Most interesting subject for the Indian Foundrymen was “Advanced Binder Technology”, which was discussed with various foundry groups.
With ever stricter environmental requirements represent an increasing challenge for the foundries, HA’s efforts to further develop Cold Box Binders have focused not only on technological optimization but also on improving environmental properties.
Besides the interesting discussions and meeting at HA group stand, Gargi HA celebrated “INDIA HOUR” on Friday, 28th June 2019 @ 1.00 pm. The function was well attended by 100 + invitees, members of IIF, Suppliers, HA worldwide and foundry industry dignitaries. It was a commitment to Indian Foundry Industry by Huttenes Albertus.
