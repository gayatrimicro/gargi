Lions Eye Care Centre 2020

 

 
Lions Club of Khopoli

Since last many years, Gargi HA is working on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities to support poor, needy and disadvantaged people in the region. This includes many initiatives such as healthcare, sanitization, education, community development, financial assistance in sports and many more.

The Lions Club of Khopoli is an active NGO at Khopoli village and we did lot of social activities with the help of Lions Club of Khopoli in the nearby schools by supporting education activities of the school children. The Lions Club of Khopoli approached us with an idea of having Eye Care Centre at Khopoli and we promised to support this social cause.

There are lack of medical facilities at Khopoli village near our factory. In the past we did many medical camps at nearby villages in Khopoli with the help of Lions Club of Khopoli. Around the Khopoli village there is no good eye care center and villagers have to travel to other city for minor eye checkup. After taking an experience from the medical camps, Mr. Vinod Kapur - Chairman Emeritus of Gargi HA, put before the Board the request of Lions Club of Khopoli of having an Eye Care Centre at Khopoli and the Board Members immediately supported the cause.

The dream of having State of Art Eye Care Centre at Khopoli came true when the Lions Eye Care Centre donated by Gargi HA, was inaugurated on 08/10/2020 with the help of financial support by Gargi HA. The Lions Eye Care Centre will provide services such as eye examination, treatments and cataract surgeries will be held in this eye care center. All these services will be provided to poor and needy ones at very low cost.

The Gargi HA will continue with its social activities in the region by taking more projects and support the nearby regions as part of its CSR activities.
