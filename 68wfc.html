Mega Event – 68 th World Foundry Congress, Chennai
 

After 21 years, the 68th World Foundry Congress came to India for 2008. This year, the Congress was combined with the 56th Indian Foundry Congress and IFEX 2008 International Foundry Exhibition at Chennai, from the 7th through the 10th of February 2008.This event, which was well organized by The Institute of Indian Foundrymen, served to provide an excellent opportunity for the foundry fraternity to exchange technology ideas, share current developments in metal castings and to firm up business relations.

The theme of this year’s Congress was “Cast for Competitive Edge,” which emphasized cost saving ideas which also supported the importance of quality of production. Today, India is positioned as 4th in world casting production, with 7.17 million tons casting production in the year 2006, up from the previous 6th place ranking in world casting production statistics. This phenomenal growth is propelled by the increasing demand of auto and engineering applications produced in the country.
