  A New Tabloid in the Foundry Industry.
 

 

Foundry Review, India’s first new-age international tabloid on the foundry industry was launched in Pune at the hands of veteran foundry industrialist Vinod Kapur, Vice President, World Foundry Organisation & CMD, Gargi Huttenes-Albertus Pvt. Ltd., Bina Verma, Pulisher and Editor of the tabloid and MD of Asian Industry & Information Services Pvt Ltd (AllSPL), received the first copy of Foundry Review from Kapur in the presence of distinguished invites from the industry including Mark Fenyes, Chairman, Omega Foundry Machinery Ltd, K S Satyanarayana, Past President, Institute of Indian Foundrymen, R V Gumaste, MD, Kirloskar Ferrous Industries Ltd and Sunil Gautam, a veteran communication advisor. A Braj Binani Group arm, AIISPL publishes leading B2B titles like Minerals & Metals Review and Construction Industry Review, besides organising various industry events.

    

(Source: Times of India, 07th September, 2012.)
