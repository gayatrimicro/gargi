Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry !! TECHNICAL SESSIONS HELD AT SHIMOGA ON 4th MARCH’13

 

IIF –Shimoga Chapter in association with M/s GARGI HUTTENES-ALBERTUS PVT LTD has organized technical sessions on Continuous mixer, Sand reclamation, PF Resins cured by Easter and Refractory coatings “Facts and Features” on 4th March 2013 at Country club of Shimoga.
 

The chairman of the chapter Mr. Damodar Baliga delivered welcome address and introduced the speakers to the Participants.
 

The Chief Guest Mr.D.S. Chandrasekhar – Hon Secretary IIF and Managing Director of M/s Shanthala Spherocast Pvt Ltd, in his address mentioned the need of such events to understand the latest foundry technologies and to practice in foundries without affecting environmental aspects and to save natural resources. He appealed the foundry personnel to come forward to share best practices among the foundries to improve profitability, to reduce production cost.
 

Mr. Chandrashekar praised Mr. Ramachar. L CET coordinator of the Shimoga chapter for conducting training classes and technical activities regularly.


In the first technical session Mr. Chris Wielding- Managing Director (Asia), M/s. Omega Foundry Machinery Ltd., UK made an excellent presentation on use of various types of Continuous Mixers and Sand Reclamation methods. He explained in detail about the advantages/ benefits of the system.
 

In the second session Mr. SID Zion-General Manager Technical, Gargi Huttenes Albertus presented on highly Alkaline PF resin cured by Ester and the advantages of the same among other resins.
 

In the last session Mr.Nishikant Ejrekar, General Manager-Technical, Gargi Huttenes Albertus-Mumbai explained in his presentation about Mould and Core Coatings and practices to be allowed to get quality castings.
 

About 100 delegates from various foundries participated in the technical sessions. Queries raised by the participants were all clarified by the speakers.
 

Finally Mr.C.Shashidhara, Dy. General Manager (Technical), Gargi Huttenes Albertus P.Ltd.,Mumbai who was an instrumental in organising this technical sessions, thanked Chief guest, President, CET Coordinator and members of the IIF Shimoga chapter and delegates for their help and cooperation.
