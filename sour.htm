The Institute of Indian Foundrymen - Southern Region celebrated Silver Jubilee Year of SOURECON 2015 at Belgaum Foundry Cluster, Belgaum on Saturday 23rd May 2015. IIF Belgaum Chapter is honoured to celebrate this Silver Jubilee year of Southern Region Conference (SOURECON 2015). The Theme of the event : "INITIATE, INNOVATE, MOTIVATE"

 

During the inauguration function, Mr. Vinod Kapur, President, World Foundry Organization graced the occasion as a Chief Guest. During his speech, he has given the details of World Foundry Congress. World Foundry Congress is held in every two years. Worldwide output of castings now exceeds 100 Million Tons.
China 44.5 Million Tons - 10th for Efficiency,
USA 12.3 Million Tons - 2nd for Efficiency,
India 9.8 Million Tons - 8th for Efficiency.

 

Hyundai strategically makes cars in India. 9.5 Million Tons from 4600 foundries. Worldwide Casting Production continued to grow in 2013, an increase of 3.4% in comparison to 2012.

Mr. Jayant Humbarwadi, Joint Managing Director, Ashok Iron Works Pvt. Ltd. was the Guest of Honour for SOURECON 2015.

 

SOURECON was attended by various chapters from IIF Southern Region like Kerala, Chennai, Coimbatore, Shimoga, Bangalore, Mangalore & Hyderabad chapter, etc. wherein total around 200 delegates attended the event.
