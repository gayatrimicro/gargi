Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry !!
Gargi HA Binders Technology Series at Mumbai

To continue having giving out of technical knowledge by conducting seminars at various Foundry Clusters, Gargi Huttenes Albertus in association with IIF-Greater Mumbai Chapter conducted Technical Symposium on Low Cost Automation and Resins & Coatings on 10th August'18 at Hotel Bawa International, Mumbai.

At the seminar, Mr. Chandan Panda, Vice President-Technical Services & Business Development, Gargi HA was the main speaker. He made a presentation on Foundry Binders and Coating Optimizing Quality, Economy & Pollution. He briefed about the quality resin process for quality castings to be followed to get the required result. It was followed by very interesting interaction session.

The Seminar was very well attended and appreciated by all participants attended.
