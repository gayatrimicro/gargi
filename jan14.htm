Felicitation Function - IIF Mumbai Chapter

 
On 5th January'14 IIF-WR and Greater Mumbai Chapter had felicitated our Managing Director, Mr. Vinod Kapur at Hotel Orchid, Mumbai, on taking over as "President of World Foundry Organization" from 1st January, 2014 for two years.
 
Mr. Vinod Kapur with IIF-WR Council members & IIF-Past Presidents, Mr. Subodh Panchal & Mrs. Prabha Kulkarni
 
Mr. Vinod Kapur is being felicitated by the Chief Guest, Mr. Prataprao Pawar, Chairman of Sakal Daily.
