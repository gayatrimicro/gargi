Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry!! Gargi HA Binder Technology Series at Mumbai / Kolhapur / Pune
By Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt.Ltd., Mumbai.

 

IIF-WR along with the Chapters of Greater Mumbai, Kolhapur and Pune conducted ‘Gargi-HA Binder Technology Series – Cast with Knowledge’ as per program:

The above Workshops were conducted by Mr. C. Shashidhara (Dy. Gen. Manager,-Technical, M/s. Gargi Huttenus Albertus Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai) and Mr. N. Ramamurthi (Sr. Foundry Expert, Ahmedabad), Mr. Raju Somani (Vice President-WR, Gargi Huttenes Albertus P. Ltd. Mumbai) assisted in replying the various queries by the participants and explaining the subject in local context.
 

Topics covered were: Classification of Binders, Types of Silica Sand and its effects, Details about Cold Box Binder Technology, Applications, Process Control, How to get optimum performance, Handling of Cold Box Binders, Trouble Shooting, Identification and remedies of casting defects attributed to sand for cast irons, etc.

The workshop was attended by 30 foundrymen at Mumbai, 75 at Kolhapur and 55 at Pune along with office bearers and council members of the chapters as well as WR.

All the workshops experienced very live interactive session and participants took advantage of the faculties’ vast knowledge. At Kolhapur chapter some of the members came with their cores to take expert advice about the problem faced. Mr.C.Shashidhara explained and given various suggestions to solve the defects.
