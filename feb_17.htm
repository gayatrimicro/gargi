Gargi HA Stall - A meeting Point for Indian & Overseas Foundrymen

Members of the Kapur Family and other team members of Gargi HA were busy welcoming the delegates and discussing about their products / technologies and exchanging thoughts and information. Mr.Gaurav Kapur & Mr. Varun Kapur and Gargi team were making special efforts to welcome Foundrymen from neighboring countries to develop deep understanding and strong business relations among the Foundrymen of this region.
