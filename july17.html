Commitment of Gargi-HA to Foundry Industry !!
Gargi HA Binders & Coatings Technology Series at Ahmedabad

Further commitment of Gargi HA to the Foundry Industry - we are glad to inform you that The Institute of Indian Foundrymen-Western Region and Ahmedabad Chapter conducted Technical Seminar on "Low Cost Automation & Sand Binder System for Foundry" on 8th July, 2017 at Hotel Courtyard Marriott, Ahmedabad.

Mr. V.P. Prem Kumar, Director, M/s. Nelcast Ltd., Chennai was Chief Guest for the event. In his brief address, he emphasised on want of Low Cost Automation and Sand Reclamation has become a very important aspect today, due to environment concern and regulation on sand mining.

The main Speakers from Gargi-HA during the event were:

1. Mr. Zion S.I.D
Title : Resin Binder Systems for Foundries.

2. Mr. Biswajit Ghosh
Title : Low Cost Automation and Sand Reclamation.

Mr. M.R. Shah was Moderator during the event.

The seminar had received an overwhelming attendance of participants, most of them being medium and small foundries from Ahmedabad, Rajkot, Bhavnagar, Vadodara and surrounding areas. The participants took active interest and interacted with both the speakers to solve their problems and to gather more information at the end of the presentations.

Mr. Vinod Kapur, Executive Chairman, Gargi HA was Special Invitee for the event. He thanked IIF-WR and Ahmedabad Chapter for hosting the seminar. Mr. Kapur also highlighted the need for such technical symposium to enhance knowledge of the Foundrymen, leading to good foundry practises, thus leading to lower rejection in turn increasing the profitability of the foundry.

He assured that Gargi HA management will always support IIF and the foundry industry with technical seminars and information at all times.
